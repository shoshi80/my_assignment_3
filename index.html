What is the typical timeline for an interior design project?

The timeline for an interior design project can vary depending on the scope and complexity of the work. On average, a small-scale project may take 4-6 weeks, while larger projects could take several months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline tailored to your specific needs.

How do you determine the cost of an interior design project?

The cost of an interior design project is influenced by factors such as the size of the space, the level of customization, materials selected, and the scope of services required. After an initial consultation and assessment of your space, we provide a detailed quote that outlines all costs associated with the project.

Can you work with my existing furniture and decor?

Absolutely! We believe in creating spaces that reflect your personality and style. We can work with your existing furniture and decor, incorporating them into the new design to create a cohesive look. If needed, we can suggest complementary pieces or adjustments to enhance the overall aesthetic.
